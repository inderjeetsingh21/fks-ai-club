üéì Learning Concepts

üß† What is Machine Learning?

Instead of programming every possible response, we give the AI examples and it learns patterns. Just like how you learned to recognize cats by seeing many cat pictures!

üìä Training Data

The question-answer pairs you create are "training data". Real AI models are trained on millions or billions of examples to become really smart!

üéØ Pattern Matching

Your chatbot looks for patterns in your question and matches it to what it learned. Different matching methods (exact, keyword, smart) work differently!

üå°Ô∏è Temperature/Creativity

Temperature controls randomness. Low = consistent answers. High = more creative/varied answers. Real AI models use this to control output!
